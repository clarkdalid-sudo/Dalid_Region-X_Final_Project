Welcome to Northern Mindanao

Northern Mindanao, also called Region X, is a diverse and vibrant area in the northern part of Mindanao island. From volcanic isles and white-sand shores in Camiguin to highland adventures in Bukidnon and impressive waterfalls and rivers in Iligan and Misamis Oriental, Region X offers unique experiences for nature lovers, culture seekers, and food adventurers.
