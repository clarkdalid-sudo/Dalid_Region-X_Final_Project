3-Day Sample Itinerary

Three day sample itinerary with times, transport and notes
Day Morning Afternoon Evening Transport Notes
Day 1 — Camiguin Arrive; island boat transfer; White Island (2–3 hrs) Sunken Cemetery view & Katibawasan Falls (lunch nearby) Seaside dinner; relax Ferry / banca Bring sun protection; check boat schedule
Day 2 — Bukidnon Travel to Bukidnon (early), Dahilayan Adventure Park — zipline Pine forest viewpoints; light trek and photos Local market or town dining Bus / private van Wear closed shoes for activities
Day 3 — Cagayan de Oro / Iligan Mapawa Nature Park — river activities (tubing, short hikes) Transfer to Iligan; Tinago Falls visit Return to CDO; sample local cuisine Private van / car Allow extra travel time for mountain roads

Travel Tips

  • Book inter-island transfers ahead during peak season.
  • Bring cash in smaller towns — ATMs can be limited.
  • Respect local customs and always ask before photographing people in traditional communities.

Packing Checklist

  • Light breathable clothes
  • Rain jacket / light poncho
  • Swimwear and quick-dry towel
  • Hiking shoes or sturdy sandals
  • Sunscreen, insect repellent, basic first-aid
